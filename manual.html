How to play
Picture Name Comments
Mouse Use the mouse to move Costello. Left-Click for initial release.
[_______] Spacebar Use Spacebar as well, to release Costello.
[P] P Hit P to pause the Game.
[M] M Hit M to mute the Game.
[ESC] ESC Within the Game, hit ESC to return to the Mainmenu.
